Google builds systems that turn large ideas into reliable services.
MapReduce made large scale data processing easier to reason about.
Workers map words into intermediate key value pairs.
The master schedules map tasks and reduce tasks across workers.
Failures happen, so production systems retry work after timeouts.
Reduce workers aggregate values and write final word counts.
Distributed systems need clear ownership, observability, and simple recovery paths.
MapReduce is a small idea with a very large impact.
